Photowalaa – Terms & Conditions

1. Booking & Payments

  • A minimum of 30% advance is required to confirm the booking.
  • 50% amount is due after the event.
  • The remaining amount must be cleared before the final delivery of photos/videos.
  • Booking is confirmed only after receipt of the advance and a signed agreement or confirmation message/email.

2. Cancellation & Refund

  • If the client cancels the booking 30 days prior to the event date, 50% of the advance will be refunded.
  • If the cancellation is made less than 30 days before the event, the advance is non-refundable.
  • In case of unforeseen circumstances from Photowalaa's side, a full refund will be issued or an alternative arrangement will be provided.

3. Travel & Accommodation

  • For outstation shoots, the client shall bear the travel, accommodation, and food expenses for the team.
  • These arrangements must be confirmed and managed at least 7 days before the event.

4. Delivery Timeline

  • Raw data (if promised) will be shared within 7-10 working days.
  • Edited photographs and cinematic videos will be delivered within 60-90 days from the last day of the event.
  • Urgent delivery requests may incur additional charges.

5. Copyright & Usage

  • Photowalaa retains the copyright to all images and videos shot.
  • We reserve the right to use selected images/videos for portfolio, website, social media, and promotional use.
  • Clients can request not to share certain private moments publicly - this must be communicated in writing at the time of booking.

6. Client Responsibilities

  • The client must coordinate schedules and ensure punctuality at the time of shoots.
  • It is the client's responsibility to assign a point of contact who will help the team coordinate during the event.
  • Meals should be arranged for the photography team during the shoot days.

7. Changes & Rescheduling

  • Any change in the event date must be informed at least 15 days prior and is subject to availability.
  • A rescheduling fee may apply in some cases.
